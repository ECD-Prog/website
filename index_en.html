About Our Club

The Model Flying Club is dedicated to the exciting hobby of model flying. We offer a range of activities for our members, including flying sessions, workshops, and events. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced flyer, our club has something for everyone.

  • Regular events and competitions

  • Exclusive membership benefits
