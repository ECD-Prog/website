Our History

The Model Flying Club was founded in 1990 by a group of passionate model flying enthusiasts. Over the years, the club has grown to become one of the most respected model flying clubs in the region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the hobby of model flying and provide a supportive community for enthusiasts of all levels. We aim to foster a love for model flying through education, events, and camaraderie.

Our Goals

  • Provide a safe and enjoyable environment for model flying
  • Offer training and workshops for beginners and advanced flyers
  • Organize regular events and competitions
  • Promote the hobby of model flying to the wider community

Founders & Key Members

Location & Facilities

We are located at Model Flying Lane 123, Anytown, Netherlands. Our facilities include a state-of-the-art airfield, a clubhouse, and workshops for building and repairing models.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of models can I fly at the club?

We welcome all types of model aircraft, including fixed-wing, helicopters, and drones. However, all models must comply with our safety guidelines.

Do I need to have my own model to join the club?

No, you do not need to have your own model to join the club. We offer training sessions and workshops where you can learn to build and fly models.

How often do you organize events?

We organize monthly events, including flying sessions, competitions, and workshops. Check our events page for the latest schedule.
